The overall digitization process of the truckload industry is still in the exploratory stage. A large number of small and medium-sized logistics companies and fleets still rely on manual completion of tasks in all steps of truckload process. FOR-U Smart Freight provides turnkey solutions to upstream and downstream entities. One such example can be seen from the services FOR-U Smart Freight provides to a leading beverage company, where we integrated our system into the client’s ERP and TMS systems, using high-quality transportation resources and intelligent services to provide overall solutions from inquiry and load tracking to receipt and settlement. In this case, vehicle operational efficiency significantly improved, while labor effectiveness increased by 30 percent.
