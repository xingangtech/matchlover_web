Corporate Social Responsibilities
FOR-U Family

It was jointly initiated by FOR-U, along with the Express Branch and the Logistics Investment and Financing Branch of the China Transportation Association, with further support from the YouChange China Social Entrepreneur Foundation.

The program includes activities such as the FOR-U Safe initiative, providing welfare security for truck carriers; the Children Times FOR-U parenting project, helping truck carriers and their kids to stay in touch; and the FOR-U Safety and Health scheme, providing health assistance to the families of truck carriers, including their parents.
