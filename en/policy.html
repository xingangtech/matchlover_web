USER PRIVACY PROTECTION RULES

To protect your privacy is very important for for-u.com. FOR-U will try the best to keep your personal information safe. Make sure you understand and know "FOR-U Users Privacy Policy" in order for FOR-U to provide you the best service. Please spend a little time and read the following terms to know how FOR-U deal with your personal information.

I. Content of the personal information FOR-U has collected

In general, you can visit our company online without telling FOR-U who you are or providing any of your personal information. However, FOR-U or FOR-U’s partners may need information from you sometimes. You can choose from a variety of situations to provide FOR-U your personal information which you can offer. For example, you can choose to provide your name, phone number or e-mail so that FOR-U can contact you and provide on-site service, etc. Before collecting your personal information, FOR-U would like you to know that how FOR-U uses this information, and get permission from you. If you provide personal information about others to FOR-U, FOR-U will assume that you have got their permission.

II. Why does FOR-U collect your personal information

The reason why FOR-U collects and uses your personal information is to provide you with the best customer service, so that you can easily enjoy FOR-U’s services and FOR-U is very pleased to accept the ideas and feedback from you. It helps in widening the range of FOR-U’ s service for you.

III. How do FOR-U collect your personal information

FOR-U will only get your personal information from feedback section of our website.Take note that FOR-U will not ask for personal information from children or self-solicit individual without permission of guardian, or in the situation of without knowing this and without the presence.

IV. How does FOR-U use your personal information

The personal information you provide to FOR-U will be used in the following purposes along with your permission:

1.To satisfy your request regarding to FOR-U’s service or website

2.Keep you in touch and allow you to participate in surveys and promotions

3.For FOR-U or selected organizations of marketing purposes

4.Data such as the number of customers for statistical analysis as anonymous

5.To enhance FOR-U’s business relationship, if you are FOR-U’s cooperation partner or business partner

V. How does FOR-U protect your personal information

When FOR-U collects information from you, we will clearly and comprehensively inform you the future situation of using this information. You can provide all or part of the information to FOR-U based on your personal preference. At the same time, you have the right to tell FOR-U which part of information can be used in which ways and which information cannot and FOR-U will respect your wishes. Our company guarantees you the security and quality of information data you provide to FOR-U with physical, electronic and other means. Take note that some countries, regions or organizations may not publish strict laws and regulations to protect your information. Within these regions or organizations, our company will still deal with your information with the way described in accordance with the terms. It is hoped you can understand that in some cases, the law requires disclosure of information. Pursuant to subpoenas of judicial or other governments, warrants, or orders, it may be needed to disclose personal information.

How do you monitor the use of personal information

In general, when FOR-U collects your personal information, we will ask for permission of using your personal information. If you have requests, FOR-U can ask for permission each time before using it. You can know the usage of your information via newspaper, network or other ways by yourself. If you find out that your personal information has been used without your permission, you can contact FOR-U by phone or online. We will investigate the origin of the problem and take protective measures to ensure the safety of your personal privacy.
